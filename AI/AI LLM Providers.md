Here’s a current, concise compendium of the major AI LLM providers, their most prominent models (as of Aug 26, 2025), plus notable “sister” assistants/agents or closely related products.

### **Global leaders**

- **OpenAI** — _Flagship_: GPT-5; o-series (o3 / o3-pro; o4-mini); also released open-weight gpt-oss-120b/20b. _Agents/assistants_: ChatGPT with **Agent Mode** (browser/terminal/app actions). 
    
- **Anthropic** — _Flagship_: Claude 3.7 Sonnet / Opus family; **Claude for Chrome** (pilot) adds a browser-using agent. 
    
- **Google (Alphabet/DeepMind)** — _Flagship_: Gemini 2.0 (experimental in Model Garden); Gemini 1.5 Pro/Flash widely deployed; **Gemini Live** conversational assistant. 
    
- **Meta** — _Flagship_: Llama 3.1 (including 405B); roadmap mentions Llama 4; **Meta AI** assistant (integrated across apps/devices). 
    
- **Microsoft** — _Flagship SLMs_: **Phi-4** family (phi-4, phi-4-reasoning, phi-4-mini-flash-reasoning); _Agents_: **Copilot** across Windows/M365; on-device APIs via Edge. 
    

---
### **Strong independents**

- **Mistral AI** — _Flagships_: Mistral Medium 3; **Codestral 2508** (code); _Assistant_: **Le Chat**. 
    
- **Cohere** — _Flagships_: **Command-A** (reasoning), Command-R/-R+; _Platform_: agentic updates in Cohere North/Workflows. 
    
- **AI21 Labs** — _Flagships_: **Jamba** (hybrid Mamba-Transformer) line incl. 1.6/1.5-Instruct; _Products_: AI21 Studio & enterprise agents. 
    
- **xAI** — _Flagship_: **Grok** (latest public: Grok-2.5 open-sourced); _Assistant_: Grok inside X (Twitter).
    
- **Reka AI** — _Flagships_: **Reka-Core**, **Reka-Flash** (multimodal); _Agents_: research & web-tasking demos. 
    
- **Databricks (Mosaic)** — _Flagship open model_: **DBRX (Base/Instruct)** (132B total-params MoE); (note: API hosting for DBRX Instruct later retired on pay-per-token, model remains available). 
    
- **NVIDIA** — _Flagships_: **Nemotron-4 340B** (Base/Instruct/Reward) for synthetic-data generation; _Platform_: NIM microservices. 
    
- **IBM** — _Flagships_: **Granite 3.x** (3.1/3.2; reasoning + vision variants); _Assistant_: **watsonx Assistant** / model gateway. 
    

---
### **China ecosystem (fast-moving)**

- **Alibaba Cloud (Qwen/Tongyi)** — _Flagships_: **Qwen2.5/3**, coder variants; _Agent frameworks_: **Qwen-Agent**, agentic benchmarks leadership reports. 
    
- **Baidu** — _Flagships_: **ERNIE 4.5** family now open-sourced (10 variants, incl. multimodal MoE); _Assistant_: **ERNIE Bot** / new agent apps. 
    
- **Tencent** — _Flagship_: **Hunyuan**; _Assistants_: **Yuanbao** & Weixin (WeChat) integrations; testing DeepSeek integration in search. 
    
- **ByteDance** — _Flagships_: **Doubao-1.5/1.6** (multimodal; realtime voice); _Assistant_: **Doubao** app with live video call features. 
    
- **DeepSeek** — _Flagships_: **DeepSeek-V3.1** and **R1** (reasoning), widely adopted/integrated. 
    
- **Moonshot AI** — _Flagship_: **Kimi 1.5+** (200K–2M-token long-context assistant). 
    
- **Zhipu AI** — _Flagship_: **GLM-4/GLM-4-Plus**; enterprise platform **BigModel**. 
    
- **SenseTime** — _Flagships_: **SenseNova 5.5** suite (multimodal); _Assistant_: **SenseChat**. 
    

---

### **Korea & Middle East**

- **NAVER** — _Flagship_: **HyperCLOVA X Think** (reasoning; Korean-centric); _Roadmap_: “unified agent” search. 
    
- **Kakao / KakaoBrain** — _Flagships_: **KoGPT** (Korean) and newer multimodal/MoE releases; _Agents_: **Kanana** AI messenger, on-device AI in KakaoTalk. 
    
- **TII (UAE) / Falcon** — _Flagships_: **Falcon 2 11B** & **11B-VLM** (open), with Falcon 3 in the works; _Assistants_: ecosystem apps. 
    
- **G42 / Inception** — _Flagships_: **JAIS 30B/70B** (Arabic-focused); _Assistant_: **JAIS Chat**. 
    

---

### **Aggregators / model gateways (useful for multi-provider access)**

- **OpenRouter** (broad marketplace of proprietary & open models). 
    
- **Azure AI Foundry** (Microsoft) hosts OpenAI, Phi-4, and others; **NVIDIA NGC/NIM** catalogs; **Hugging Face** hubs for many above.

- “Most prolific” means the lab’s _current_ flagship or most-used family in production as of **Aug 26, 2025**; links above point to official pages or reliable reporting. This list is broad but not literally exhaustive; it captures the players most organizations evaluate first today.
